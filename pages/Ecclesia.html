Ecclesia Spirit

I met Rhetta at a women’s collective in Philadelphia during the fall of 2016. I was living there to working on the presidential campaign as visual designer and fellow for NextGen Climate. What I love about Rhetta’s spiritual philosophy is that it’s rooted in compassion. Her spiritual activism initiative seeks to initate spiritually-minded people to build bridges at a time when our political atmosphere is rather toxic and uninviting. I wanted to help build that bridge by redesigning her website and coordinating an enhanced social media strategy to include her talks on Youtube.
