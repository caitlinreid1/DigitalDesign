Libreo is a free, online resource that collects relevant materials in a variety of mediums (articles, videos, and books), to introduce complex topics and encourage lifelong learning.

Unlike massive open online courses, Libreo encourages learners to develop the tools of self-study by curating the best content from a variety of sources. Future development will include point system to up-vote the best content as well as progress reports to help keep learners on track with their self-directed studies.

This app challenges the current models of finding new resourcse in a subject. Amazon organizes its content by best reviewed and best selling, but does not engage customer reviews specificially across the axis of its utility in introducing new subjects. A 5.0 rating could be garnered for entertainment value rather than uniqueness of voice and how well the author is able to articulate her points. Google, similarly, organizes its top hits according to an algorithym that deveopers try to hack using SEO techniques. Again, the content you see immediately through a search engine may not be the best resources for learning a new subject, they might just be the best marketed materials.
