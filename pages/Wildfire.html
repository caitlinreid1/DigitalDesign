Wildfire

What do the social media giants Facebook, Twitter and Tumblr all have in common? A linear timeline. Which is to say, a linear mode of experiencing their digital content. Our goal is to innovate the way user-generated content is visually depicted and ultimately consumed. On Twitter, a message can reach the world in minutes, but the scrolling news feed is a one-dimensional design. We aim to challenge the chronological model and illustrate the global flow of ideas in real time

Combining the Google Maps API and the Twitter API, our product visualizes where tweets occur, creating a compelling new way to consume social media and escape geographic and social boundaries. Social media keeps us in the know. But with newsfeeds curated by algorithms, we often find ourselves in the echo chambers of a like-minded bubble.
