Soel Farm

Soel Farm is a family-run business based in Millstone, New Jersey. The owners are professionals who, at the peak of their careers decided to move to a farm in New Jersey, transforming their enthusiastic hobby into a new way of living. While specializing in microgreens, Soel Farm grows microgreens, vegetables, chickens and sheep. Microgreens are young seedlings of vegetables, herbs, beans and grains. Unlike sprouts, they require both soil and sunlight, taking a bit longer to grow. As a result, they are exceptionally nutritional and provide excellent source of health-supporting phytonutrients.Despite their small size, microgreens have surprisingly intense flavors and are used as a visual enhancement and flavor accent. Fine dining restaurants favor microgreens to enhance both the creative presentation and flavor of their dishes.
